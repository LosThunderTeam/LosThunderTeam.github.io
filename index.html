Key Features


Multiplayer

This mode allows you to go head-to-head with other players as you battle it out to be victorious.

Quiz

This educational aspect of the game allows players to build upon their knowledge as well as learn new information. Answering the question is the key to unlocking the doors

Leaderboard

This element adds a competitive nature to the game. It allows members to be able to view high scores and have a goal of being number 1 on the list.

How it works

This magical school game is perfect for kids who have are part of KS3 (11-14 year olds), the players will be able to play against their friends or anyone online on the server. The game is played with the dedicated controls; each player will be able to navigate through the various obstacles along the way with the aim to reach the door first. Once at the door a player reaches a door, they will face a question (either Maths or English) which will determine whether a player can carrying navigating the map or will have to attempt another question if their answer was wrong. However, reaching the door first doesn't guarantee victory, the winner of the game is the player who bypasses all the obstacles as well as questions and reaches the finish line first. The educational aspect of the game is very important because it challenges the players as well as adding a fun element to the game.
